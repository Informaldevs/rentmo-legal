## 1. Intended Users and Age Requirement

Rentmo is intended **only for users who are at least eighteen (18) years old**.

We do not knowingly collect, use, or process personal data from individuals under 18. If we become aware that personal data of a minor has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

### Property and Tenant Data

## 3. How We Use Information

Personal data is used to:
- Provide and operate Rentmo features

- Send service-related notifications
- Improve app security and performance
