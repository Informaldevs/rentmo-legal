# Rentmo – Privacy Policy

**Version:** 2025-02  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

Rentmo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal information in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and app store requirements.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Name
- Email address
- Mobile number
- Encrypted authentication credentials
- Profile details and preferences

### Usage Information
- Device and app interaction data
- Log files and analytics
- Diagnostic and performance data

### Property and Tenant Data
- Property, room, and bedspace details
- Tenant names and contact information
- Rental amounts, payments, and utilities

Users control all property and tenant data they upload.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use data to:
- Provide and operate Rentmo features
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Manage properties, tenants, payments, and utilities
- Send service-related notifications
- Improve app performance and security

We do not sell personal data.

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing is based on:
- User consent
- Contractual necessity
- Legitimate interests such as security and fraud prevention

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Data may be shared with:
- Cloud hosting providers
- Authentication, SMS, and email services
- Analytics tools
- Payment processors (for subscriptions)
- Authorities when required by law

---

## 5. Subscriptions and Payments

If you subscribe to paid features:
- Payments are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store
- Rentmo does not store payment card details
- Subscription management and refunds are handled by the app store provider

---

## 6. Data Retention and Account Deletion

Data is retained only as long as necessary.

You may delete your account at any time through the app or by contacting support. Upon deletion:
- Personal data and user-created records are permanently removed
- Active sessions are invalidated
- Some data may be retained if legally required

---

## 7. Data Security

We apply reasonable safeguards such as encryption, access controls, and secure infrastructure. No system can guarantee absolute security.

---

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access and correct your data
- Request data export
- Object to processing
- Delete your account and personal data
- File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

Rentmo is not intended for users under 18 years of age.

---

## 10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use means acceptance of the updated version.
